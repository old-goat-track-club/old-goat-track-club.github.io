About Us

Initially founded as an alumni club from the Worcester Polytechnic Institute track and cross country teams, the OGTC has expanded to include alumni from many Northeast USA universities and clubs. The team has rapidly grown over the past few years and is always welcoming of new members.

Members of the club actively participate in indoor and outdoor track and field meets, road races (including the USATF NE Road Grand Prix), and the USATF New England XC Grand Prix series. If it's a race in the greater Boston area, we have someone racing it! Additionally the club hosts the Old Goat Track Club XC 5k meet in August which opens the XC Grand Prix each fall.

The mission of the Old Goat Track Club is to combine dedication, development and competitiveness with lasting friendships, inclusiveness, and camaraderie. Every athlete irrespective of experience and location will find a welcoming community dedicated to shared goals and support. We aim to carry on and extend the legacy of the WPI Track and Field and Cross Country community by striving for excellence at a national level. We embrace the joy of running and athletics as a collective pursuit, valuing each member's journey and dedication to individual growth within a team environment.


The Old Goat Track Club is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization
